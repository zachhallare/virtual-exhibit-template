## **Introduction**

<br />

This interactive exhibit lets you explore and compare two dominant computer architectures — **ARM** and **x86** — through a flashcard-based interface. Click on either architecture to browse categories like **Overview**, **Performance**, **Use Cases**, **Key Devices**, **Pros and Cons**, and a side-by-side **Comparison Table**. Each card flips to reveal detailed information, animated charts, and interactive lists, making it easy to understand the differences between RISC and CISC design philosophies at your own pace.
